In the vast multiverse we refer to colloquially as the ‚ÄúAstral,‚Äù there are vast multitudes of locales and beings alike. While many such beings are entirely unique there are some things that can be used to more easily categorize and understand them. Perhaps most universal of these aspects is what I refer to as ‚ÄúPolarity.‚Äù Polarity is a sliding scale that has little to nothing to do with how ‚Äúhigh above us,‚Äù a given being is, and everything to do with the general tendency of their energies. Simply put, this scale has Light/Order/Creation/Positivity at one pole, and Darkness/Chaos/Destruction/Negativity at the other. Virtually all beings are closer to one side or another of this scale, though a vast majority are somewhere in the middle with leanings one way or the other. It should be noted that These things should not under any circumstances, be directly correlated with our human ideas of good and evil. Beings primarily associated with light and order can be just as harmful as the alternative. With that idea aside, I will move on to a few common varieties that can be encountered in the Astral.

Vultures

More of a functional designation than a specific variety of entity, Vultures are--typically--lower end beings that prey--mostly--on the vulnerable. As such new Practitioners or those unable to detect the beings, or unable to defend themselves from them, may find themselves falling prey to such an entity. These can work either as a group or individually to harass, harm, or siphon energy from Practitioners. Once an individual has learned how to form barriers and/or combat such beings, they typically become trivial to deal with and by and large will cease bothering the individual. These beings come in two semi-distinct varieties: Intelligent and Feral. The former tend to be weak higher entities that rely on the vulnerable to sustain or enrich themselves energetically. These beings are capable of rational thought and reasoning, but are not prone to negotiating with their victims. However, they are at least smart enough to recognize when their efforts are no longer effective and--if left to their own devices after a victim has learned to defend themselves--will generally take their leave to find someone more vulnerable. The other variety, Feral, are entities without rational thought who function more like animals than sapient beings, simply preying on the vulnerable as a predator on prey beasts. A significant show of force or other means of intimidation or prevention will often dissuade such beings from bothering a practitioner. Though, these beings are very sensitive to vulnerability so if someone other than their target wards them off, they may simply wait till they see another opportunity to strike.

Parasites

Similar to Feral Vultures, Parasites are typically non-sapient entities that operate by instinct. However, unlike Vultures, these often disgusting pests do not attack their prey, but instead parasitize them. The easiest way to differentiate them from Vultures is in the fact that Vultures will attack you like a predator might, whereas Parasites will attach themselves to their target and attempt to conceal themselves while they slowly feed. Some may burrow into the astral body, whereas others will feed by metaphysically attaching themselves to the outside of the Subtle body. Others still may create a sort of energetic thread that they can siphon energy through. These entities--given the sustained nature of their feeding--can even have physically noticeable effects including unexplainable pain, discomfort, oddly prominent and consistent nightmares, a general sense of dread, and even the dulling of certain or all emotion. As a result of the many side effects of their presence, Parasites are best gotten rid of as soon as they are identified to be present. Notably, unlike Vultures, Parasites can and will target any Practitioner or target that they can attach to--regardless of method. This, combined with the deleterious effects of their continued feeding, and the fact that they can conceal themselves from detection makes them a particularly frustrating form of being to contend with. If you ever have the misfortune of interacting with energetic parasites--be it helping another get rid of them, or them feeding on your person--ensure that a third party scans you remotely and likely assists you in clearing any remaining instances of the pests. This is exceptionally important as Parasites can sometimes either attract more of their own kind to a host, reproduce, or even split into many parts allowing portions of them to be cleared while they regenerate within the host and continue to feed. Higher Selves

Owed a section of their own the term 'Higher Self' is used by the spiritual community to refer to two distinct phenomena. As such this section exists to dispel any confusion regarding these two use cases.

Type 1 - The Oversoul

This type, renamed for ease of reference, is colloquially called many other things with some examples being 'Higher Self,' 'Collective,' or even 'Intuition.' In essence, the Oversoul is the culmination of all of your soul's incarnations thus far and when accessed can provide wisdom from said overarching consciousness. As such this form of the higher self can provide insight into one's path, general guidance in life, as well as providing a stronger intuition for any number of other things spiritual and otherwise. While it is technically a consciousness, it is typically not experienced as a distinct being, but more as a general intuition or 'sense' for things. Sometimes people will experience it as a distinct being, but in such cases it will by and large be nameless and faceless, serving more as a more familiar medium to experience something through.

Type 2 - Incarnations

While this use of ‚ÄòHigher Self‚Äô or ‚ÄòHigher Selves‚Äô is less common‚Äîin my experience‚Äîin spiritual communities, it is nonetheless one worth expanding upon. Simply put, this variety of Higher Self is used to denote other Incarnations of a given soul, both those that once lived (Past Selves/Past Lives), those that yet live (Parallel Lives), and those who will live (Future Selves/Future Lives). Notably, these are relative to our linear experience of time, which due to the generally non-linearity of time in the Astral can create what might appear to be inconsistencies. To explain this it should be noted that higher beings in particular tend not to experience time as we do and can thus interact both in the ‚Äòpresent‚Äô as we understand it and in their ‚Äòpast‚Äô or ‚Äôfuture‚Äô should they so desire. This variety of Higher Self can be considered as individuals for the most part and will interact with us on a comparable level to such. These beings can hinder or help if they so choose, though by and large they will not do anything that would permanently harm‚Äîat least when interacting with fellow Incarnations.

Common Species

A section dedicated to specific subsets of beings that can be encountered in the Astral or that one might have kinship with through their spiritual heritage.

Dragons

Characterized by their grandiose presence, both metaphysically and through their raw aura, Dragons--while perhaps not common, in the same sense as Vultures or Parasites, are a category of being that is certainly not uncommon to encounter in the Astral. Making their home throughout the cosmos, Draconic beings take on numerous shapes and possess a wide variety of capabilities and temperaments. They are primarily unified by their distinct presence, their strong sense of self, and perhaps most importantly their distinct energy signatures, which--with very few exceptions--almost always includes something I have come to refer to as "Dragon Fire." To expand on this point, "Dragon Fire," is--in its base manifestation--an energy of purification. Unlike some energies which have very narrow applications, Dragon Fire is innately flexible as its precise effects depend on the intent of its wielder. Typically it will naturally "purify" other energies, either returning them to an unaltered or uncorrupted state, or transmuting them into aspectless energy without any intent or otherwise effect. Notably, when dealing with dragons it is best to act with respect, if not for their power--which is often considerable--than simply as a matter of course. This is generally wise, as Dragons are often proud beings, and while they are not typically arrogant, it is simply better to be on their good side than not.

Fae

Like Dragons, the Fae are an energetically and cosmetically diverse species who are defined not so much by a shared racial appearance, but rather by a subset of other traits. Namely, Fae are innately tricksters and--generally--they operate by a series of rules, and a form of morality, that are by and large alien to us as human Practitioners. Notably, while some Dragons can be found on higher densities on Earth, Fae exist here in abundance. As a result, they are not terribly difficult to encounter. However, unlike Dragons, the Fae do not treat with Humans on the same terms, instead interacting with us through their own labyrinthine set of rules and alien logic that can be difficult to puzzle out. When treating with the Fae, things to keep in mind are as follows:
  • Maintain an air of politeness as any perceived slight can earn you the long term ire of the Fae and they are wont to hold grudges.
  • If asked a question, make sure you think before responding as Fae are well known for their capacity to bind others into contracts or exchanges without our express awareness.
  • Never assume that any given Fae has your best interests at heart, their motives are cloudy at best, and alien and hostile at worst. Kindness and pleasant demeanor does not equate to goodness or even genuine intentions.
  • Do not accept gifts from the Fae, because they can and often will think of these gifts as trades, in which you may well owe them favors.
  • Bargain with the Fae at your own peril.
Beyond this, it is worth noting that Fae--as a rule--will not lie. It is not simply that they choose not to, but rather that they cannot as lies are not only treated with unbelievable disdain in their culture, but also carry with them extreme karmic weight. Any Fae who breaks their word--though this must be to the letter, rather than the spirit--will often face very significant backlash as a result. In terms of universal abilities, the Fae excel in the creation of contracts, the use of Sigils, and perhaps most notably, tend to have a total mastery of Glamours. These pseudo-real constructs are essentially illusions with some limited substance. However, the more observers believe a given Glamour to be true, the more real they become. As such, do not think that just because a Fae is utilizing illusion that those illusory creations cannot harm you. Furthermore, their consistent use of illusions means that you cannot always trust your senses when treating with them. As a result, tread with caution. (edited)

Polarized Entities

To return to the idea of polarity, there are some entities that are somewhat distinct in terms of the general inclinations of their energy. To reiterate, such beings are neither inherently good nor evil and‚Äîas with any entity that you do not know‚Äîshould be treated with equal care and caution. Furthermore, any information provided here should be considered to follow the law of averages in that it is not universal, just most commonly encountered.

Negative Polarity

Beings on this side of the sliding scale are more "negatively" aspected, their energies gravitating more towards destruction, 'darkness,' and/or chaos. They tend to have tendencies aligning with these aspects and though this does not make them inherently evil, they should be treated with care and caution. (edited)

Demons

Perhaps the most 'familiar,' if not the most commonly encountered of Negatively Polarized entities, the term 'Demon' is actually something of a blanket term used by Practitioners to define any being that is almost entirely negatively aspected, energy-wise. While this is less universal, my experiences with Demonic entities--corroborated with other Practitioners I've interacted with--is such that there are actually two to three distinct varieties of these entities. There are Abrahamic Demons, which are those relating to the known abrahamic religions--these are often collective entities that have been partially, or wholly, created through the confluence of belief and existing energies in the astral. Some few of these Demons are preexisting entities who--to varying degrees--align with the common mythologies to which their name(s) are associated. Second are 'lesser demons,' which are less actual demons and more just darkly aspected beings that many Practitioners refer to as such. This often includes beings like imps and other similarly 'weak,' but negatively aspected entities. Last, but not least of the three, are what I personally refer to as True Demons, which hail from a set of realms that they say roughly translate to the "Twisted Realms." This subset of planes are one of the likely inspirations for the ideas surrounding "Hell" or other similarly punitive afterlives as they are ruled by demonic entities, steeped in chaos, and filled to brimming with violence and deceit. While such beings are--once again--not distinctly 'evil,' they can be considered to not value certain characteristics that we as humans would consider good or positive. At best they have variations of said concepts (such as "love," for example) that while not exact parallels at least serve--for them--a similar purpose. (edited)

Kahlvenim

Beings originating from a place commonly referred to--by beings I've interacted with and myself--as the Dark Realm, the Khalvenim are to Demons as Demons are to us. Simply put, they are the most 'negatively' aspected group of beings I've yet to encounter in the Astral. These beings do not often depart their own realms, but nonetheless they bear mentioning if only such that those who read this can be aware of them and so be wary of interaction. Notably, Kahlvenim, or Dark Realm entities tend to be significantly more dangerous than their Twisted Realm or Demonic counterparts. This is chiefly because they are, on average, far more darkly aspected than even Practitioners with such leanings. Their energy is dense, corruptive, and hungry as a rule and their attitudes are such that they are wont to charm, deceive, and entrap anyone they can feasibly access. If they should decide not to do such, often is it that they may taunt, torment, or otherwise harass Practitioners. Additionally, unlike Demons, which can often be warded off by some of the standard defensive techniques--Barriers, the "White Light," methods as two examples--Kahlvenim are comprised of and empowered with such negatively aspected energy that they can corrupt or cut through other energies unless they are particularly potent (either in being polarized very far to the other side of the sliding scale, or just generally very potent). As such, unless a Practitioner is particularly skilled and confident in their ability to deal with such threats, they should steer clear of the Dark Realms as typically the Khalvenim will not stray far outside of their own planes.

Positive Polarity

As one might expect, beings on this end of the sliding scale are composed of, utilize, and/or are aligned with 'Positively' aspected energy. As a result their energies, and their tendencies in general are moreso aligned with Order, Creation, Light, and Positivity. While beings of this nature are generally helpful, they can be dangerous to those who are themselves darkly aspected as they tend to be diametrically opposed to such forces. Additionally, as with beings of Negative Polarity, they are neither inherently good nor bad as we would consider such things and so should be treated with caution and care until they are a known quantity.

Angels

As with Demons, Angels are a well known "Positively polarized" entity, being strictly aspected towards Light and Order--and sometimes Creation. While the temperament and exact abilities of angels differs wildly, they are often possessed of power capacities when it comes to the purification or destruction of negatively aspected energies. Furthermore, while there are certainly exceptions, most Angels are diametrically opposed to beings of negative polarity and as such interactions with such can lead to conflict or--at minimum--an abrasive attitude. Notably there are many varieties of Angel and some are stranger than others, which can result in miscategorization as often people expect to encounter winged humanoids, though this is hardly the only form that Angels take. Lastly, it should be kept in mind that not all angels are the abrahamic sort.

Lithari (The Ascendant)

A subset of entities hailing from what they refer to as the Light Realms, the Lithari are beings of absolute Creation, Order, Light, and Positivity. They have little--if any--capacity to accept or sometimes even conceive of things on the other end of the sliding scale. This results in them being diametrically opposed to energies, entities, and realms that are darkly aspected. Nonetheless, Lithari are almost universally non-violent as violence typically requires some degree of destruction. As a result they are generally relatively safe to interact with, though there are always exceptions. Notably, if you have significantly 'negative' ties and kinship in your soul lineage it may not be entirely wise to interact with these beings. Otherwise, they are a wonderful source of knowledge when it comes to purification, Light, Creation, and other Positively Aspected energies and concepts. Nonetheless, as with any new being, members of this class of entity should be treated with caution and care
